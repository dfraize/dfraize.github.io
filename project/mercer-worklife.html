Mercer - Worklife

This was a small project to launch a product that would allow users to view there government filings, investments and manage other aspects of their pension plan. There was also an effort to white label this product.

My Role
UI Developer
Project Team
1 UX Designer, 1 Visual Designer and 2 UI Developers
Duration
5 Months
Project Status
Went live into production
Design Methodologies
Work with project stakeholders to collect requirements. Spent time working through design interations and reviews with stakeholders. Once we had a version 1 of the product we pulled in the development team to have review sessions. Changes from of these reviews where incorporated into final designs.
Business Goals
Bringing a new product to the market for easier analysis of a user's pension plan. Centralize things in one place for a more holistic view and management of pension plan.
Actual Results
Product was well received and was completed on time and on budget.
